Protocol 1

CORE: Stabilization & Baseline

The CORE phase is designed for clients just starting their GLP-1 journey or those currently experiencing moderate to severe side effects (nausea, fatigue, severe appetite suppression). The goal is Harm Minimization and Movement Re-integration.

  • Training: Focus on gentle exercises for 15-20 min, 3x per week. For the more chalenging days we offer "Movement Snacks" (1-2 min, 5-10x per day). This includes walking, gentle stretching, and light band work. We prioritize maintaining daily activity, not intensity.
  • Nutrition: Strict focus on hitting minimum protein and hydration targets. We introduce protein-forward snacks and easy-to-digest whole foods to manage nausea.
  • Key Metric: Side effect reduction and consistent daily steps.

ELEVATE: Muscle Preservation

ELEVATE is the comprehensive 12-week program designed for clients who are ready to commit to active muscle preservation. It incorporates CORE's 4-week stabilization protocol to safely adapt your body, then transitions directly into 8 weeks of structured resistance training to maximize fat loss and retain lean muscle mass.

  • Training: 3x per week Full-Body resistance training using dumbbells or home equipment. Focus on compound movements (squats, rows, presses) with appropriate intensity Relative Perceived Effort (RPE) 7-8.
  • Nutrition: Advanced protein timing and supplementation strategies to ensure peak muscle synthesis despite lower caloric intake.
  • Key Metric: Lean body mass (LBM) stabilization and strength increase (measured by RPE and poundage lifted).
Protocol 3

APEX: High End 1:1 Coaching

This premium, coach-led protocol is for clients dedicated to achieving peak body recomposition through total accountability. The program uses a customized dashboard for daily data collection, allowing your coach to review key metrics weekly and precisely adjust your training in real-time for safe and accelerated results.

  • Training: Your workouts are tailored daily to your current ability, equipment access (bodyweight, home weights, or full gym), and measured recovery. The coach utilizes your dashboard metrics to morph your training volume and intensity weekly, ensuring the safest and quickest path to your body composition goals.
  • Nutrition: Your coach provides daily oversight on critical hydration and protein targets, monitoring your intake to support recovery. Leveraging your mandatory weekly video call, we collaboratively review your full macronutrient trends (carbs/fats) and preferred food choices to build the safest, most effective fueling strategy for peak performance and accelerated fat loss.
  • Key Metric: Success is tracked collaboratively using a custom dashboard collecting daily inputs on mood, fatigue, sleep quality, GI status, nausea and performance. This data is reviewed by your coach in a mandatory weekly video call to guarantee continuous progression and preemptively address any risk of overtraining or injury.

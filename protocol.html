CORE — 4-Week Stabilization

CORE is a considerate introduction for women beginning GLP-1 therapy or those needing a gentler training return. Over four weeks we prioritize symptom control, preserved strength, and consistent movement patterns so you feel secure and steady.

  • Training: Short, daily movement sessions (15–25 minutes), mobility, and low-load resistance to maintain neuromuscular connection without provoking nausea.
  • Nutrition: Gentle fueling strategies focused on protein-forward snacks and the Fed State Mandate (pre-workout carbs when needed) to reduce energy dips.
  • Recovery: Simple sleep, hydration, and stress-management practices to keep your autonomic balance calm and supportive of training.

ELEVATE — CORE + 8 Weeks Strength (12 Weeks Total)

ELEVATE picks up where CORE leaves off. After establishing a comfortable, stable baseline, we layer in progressive resistance and structured volume to preserve and build lean muscle with elegance and precision.

  • Training: Three full-body sessions per week (dumbbells or home equipment) focused on compound lifts, deliberate tempo, and progressive overload.
  • Nutrition: Advanced protein timing and targeted carbohydrate placement around training to protect performance and minimize side effects.
  • Coaching: Weekly check-ins and program tweaks to maintain steady progress without overwhelming your system.

My Story: Why RenewFit Exists

Like many, I see the powerful potential of GLP-1 medications to help people achieve significant weight loss. However, after researching the effects of these drugs since they first hit the market, I recognized a critical, overlooked risk: **losing muscle along with fat.**

My philosophy is built on one core truth: GLP-1s are a powerful tool, not a magic pill, and they are not a replacement for a healthy lifestyle.

RenewFit was founded to bridge that gap—to provide structured, science-backed fitness programming that works *with* your medication to ensure the weight you lose is fat, and the results you gain are sustainable for life.

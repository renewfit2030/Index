Choose the Guidance That Fits Your Phase

Our protocols are designed for continuous adaptation. Start safely with mobility exercies, and scale up seamlessly to active strength building as your body—and your GLP-1 journey—evolves.

CORE

4-Week Foundation

"This 4-week program is purely defensive, focused on preventing muscle loss while your body adapts to the medication's effects. It establishes baseline movement and habits that prepare you for the strength demands of the ELEVATE plan, while minimizing nausea and fatigue."

View CORE

ELEVATE

12-Week Strength Program

This 12-week program incorporates the CORE plan's foundations and builds on them with targeted resistance training (using dumbbells or gym equipment). The primary goal is aggressive Sarcopenia Defense and active muscle preservation, allowing you to build strength thoughtfully and safely with structured workouts that support energy, metabolism, and sustainable progress.

View ELEVATE

APEX

1:1 Coaching

This coach-led protocol is reserved for clients who are fully dedicated to making permanent, holistic lifestyle changes, not just short-term weight loss. It provides 1:1 accountability to master the exercise, nutrition, and recovery habits necessary for long-term body recomposition.

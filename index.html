Our Mission Statement
Muscle Loss Risk

Rapid weight loss often strips away essential lean muscle (Sarcopenia). We prioritize strength and protein to defend against this and maintain your metabolism.

Nausea & Fatigue

Traditional workouts can trigger severe nausea and fatigue. Our programs use "Movement Snacks" and appropriate rest to work with your medication, not against it.

Protein Challenge

Appetite suppression makes hitting protein goals tough. Every plan integrates specific, actionable nutrition guidance for muscle recovery and preservation.

Our Three Personalized Protocols

RenewFit is scientifically designed for your GLP-1 journey. Click below to see which protocol is best for you.

CORE

The Stabilization Phase

This 4-week program is purely defensive, focused on preventing muscle loss while your body adapts to the medication's effects. It establishes baseline movement and habits that prepare you for the strength demands of the ELEVATE plan, while minimizing nausea and fatigue.

View CORE Protocol

ELEVATE

Stabliziation & Preservation Phases

This 12-week program incorporates the Core plan and builds on it with targeted resistance training (dumbbells or gym). The goal is aggressive Sarcopenia Defense and active muscle preservation.

View ELEVATE Protocol

APEX

The Total Recomposition & Accountability Phase

This coach-led protocol is for clients who are fully dedicated to making permanent, holistic lifestyle changes, not just short-term weight loss. It provides 1:1 accountability to master the exercise, nutrition, and recovery habits necessary for long-term body recomposition.
